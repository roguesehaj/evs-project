About Us

We are a dedicated team of environmental science students, passionate about raising awareness on the critical issue of plastic pollution. Our goal is to educate communities, promote sustainable habits, and inspire individuals to take actionable steps towards a cleaner planet.

Our project "From Convenience to Consequences" highlights the journey of plastic from its beneficial uses to the devastating environmental impacts. Join us in our mission to make the world a greener, cleaner place for future generations.

Contact us at info@plasticawareness.org to learn more about our project or to get involved.
